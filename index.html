FAQ

Common Questions

How accurate is the AI diagnosis?

+

PlantDoctor uses the latest Gemini AI technology, reaching over 98% accuracy for common garden diseases and pests when a clear photo is provided.

Can it identify indoor plants?

+

Yes! Our model is trained on both outdoor crops and popular indoor species like Monstera, Pothos, and Succulents.

Are the organic treatments safe for pets?

+

Most of our organic recommendations use common kitchen ingredients (like baking soda or neem oil). We always provide safety warnings for each treatment.

Is there an offline mode?

+

The AI analysis requires an internet connection to reach our cloud-based model, but you can view your scan history offline at any time.
